Tracy Osborn is a designer, developer, author, and entreprenerd living in Toronto, Canada.

Tracy was born in California, taught herself how to build websites when she was 12, and holds a Bachelors of Fine Arts degree in Art & Design.

In 2007, she taught herself how to program and launched WeddingLovely, which was accepted to 500 Startup's incubator in Fall 2011. She's also the author, designer, and publisher of Hello Web App, a self-published book series on beginner web app development with Python and Django.

In her spare time, she is an avid outdoorswoman, spending as much time as possible hiking, climbing, rafting, mountaineering, surfing, and anything else outdoorsy.
